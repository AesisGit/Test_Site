What makes us different?


BASTION has brought a new Psychology to the Emergency Response Driver and Medical Training to the Emergency Services industry and are proud to be working with ProQual to deliver this to you. BASTION has available a suite of specialist Emergency Response Driver and Medical Qualifications designed and delivered by specialist Emergency Response Driver Trainers and Medical Trainers.


As you would expect, all of our trainers have extensive on-road 'blue light'​ experience from Emergency backgrounds or the Military. We truly believe that this makes us the best at what we do at BASTION. We also work closely with both serving and ex-Police, so we know we can back up our claims with what we have set out to achieve, we are the "Specialist Training Provider to the Emergency Services".


BASTION is able to deliver both Emergency Response Driver and Emergency Medical Training to those in the healthcare industry, both private and commercial.
