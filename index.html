What makes us different?


UKERT bring a new era of Emergency Response Driver and Medical Training to the Emergency Services industry and working with ProQual, UKERT has developed a suite of specialist Emergency Response Driver and Medical Qualifications designed and delivered by specialist Emergency Response Driver Trainers and Medical Trainers.


As you would expect all of our trainers have extensive on-road 'blue light' experience from Emergency backgrounds or the Military. We truly believe that this makes us the best at what we do at UKERT. We work closely with both serving and ex-Police, so we know we can back up our claims with what we have set out to achieve, we are "Specialist Training Provider to the Emergency Services".


UKERT is able to deliver both Emergency Response Driver and Emergency Medical Training to those in the healthcare industry, both private and commercial.
